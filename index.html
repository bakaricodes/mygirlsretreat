About My Girl's Retreat

Some words about us

My Girl’s Retreat is a yearlong enrichment program designed to build relationships between adolescent girls and their peers.

My Girl’s Retreat centers on the dedication and commitment of community members, and volunteers who promote health, wellness and academic excellence by serving as resources, sponsors, and mentors.

It’s a place for girl’s to relax, reflect and recharge the soul.

  • MISSION - to create a community for adolescent girls to improve their lives by developing strategies for the fulfillment of goals and achievements.

How it works

Monthly Commitment

One weekend per month the girls will come together, and begin their weekend retreat.

MGR Pledge

Each girl must complete a commitment pledge which confirms her willingness, and commitment to participate.

Parent/Guardian Support

Parents must complete the check in process before leaving the participant. Check in time is 7pm Friday evening. Check out time is 12pm Sunday afternoon.

MGR Rules

Cell phones, and other electronic devices are not allowed during the weekend retreat.

My Girl's Retreat Eligibility Criteria

Must be enrolled in grades 6-12 at the time of application.
Must have a recommendation from a parent, teacher, mentor, or community member.
Must be willing to commit to at least one weekend a month to the program.
